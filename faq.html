About Barter

Barter is the world’s first mainstream cryptocurrency-powered marketplace. Our mission is simple: give people the freedom to buy & sell online — safely, anonymously, and without restrictions.

Our Vision

We believe in decentralization. Barter is designed for the people — no ID, no banks, and no invasive tracking. Whether you’re buying a digital or physical product we want your experience to be seamless, private, and borderless.

How Does It Work?

  • Crypto-Powered: We support all major cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and Pi. Use a third party crypto wallet (i.e. Coinbase) to load your account balance or pay for orders.
  • Built-in Escrow: Funds are held securely until both buyer and seller complete the trade.
  • Anonymous: No identity verification, no credit cards, no problem.
  • Decentralized: We're working toward decentralized hosting and smart contract-based trade processing in future updates.

Our purpose is to provide the ability to list digital or physical products for sale, choose your preferred crypto to obtain, and receive your coin of choice from us—even if the buyer pays with a different coin. We charge a small seller's fee of 8% (6% for early-access users). Use a third party wallet (i.e. Coinbase) to load account balance or directly pay for orders. Together, we can create the world's first community of buyers and sellers that revolves around the crypto world.

Common Questions

Is Barter really anonymous?

Yes. We never ask for personal details, and we’re building with privacy-first design. Trades are verified by wallet, not identity.

What currencies can I use?

Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and Pi will be supported at launch. More options may follow based on community demand.

When will Barter launch?

We’re actively building the MVP now. Early access is expected in 2025. Subscribers will be the first to know!

Will Barter take a cut of my sales?

Yes, but transparently. A small transaction fee will help us cover infrastructure and support continued development.

Can I sell anything?

We’ll support a wide range of legal goods and services. Barter won’t allow anything that violates international law, but aims to maximize seller freedom. Think of us as a legal Silk Road.
